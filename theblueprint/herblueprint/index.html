What is UGC? (And Why It's Your Perfect Start)

UGC, or User Generated Content, is the secret weapon for making money online without the pressure of being a full-time "influencer."

User Generated Content (UGC)

  • You create content (photos/videos) FOR a brand to use on THEIR social media pages.
  • No need to post on your own page. Your privacy is protected.
  • Follower count does not matter. Brands care about your authenticity, not your audience size.
  • Perfect for a flexible side-hustle or as a first step into content creation.

Influencer Marketing

  • You create content and post it to YOUR OWN social media pages to promote a brand.
  • Your follower count and engagement can lead to higher pay.
  • Helps you build a personal brand and a community around your content.
  • This guide teaches you how to grow into this for even higher earning potential!
